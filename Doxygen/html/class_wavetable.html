uint16_t Wavetable::wtLength
private

The wtLength was a tricky one to find and reason upon. A compromise must be found between a reasonable length or size in KB for it to fit into at least L2 cache but still be able to give a good resolution for the "compressed" samples. Samples will later be retrieved via linear interpolation.

A WT size of 2^12, or 4096 (opimizing the range by using a power of two), is calculated as follows:

WaveTableLength * 8 bytes of precision (double) / 1024 = 32KB
